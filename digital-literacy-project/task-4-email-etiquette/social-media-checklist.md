# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
1. **Do curate a professional image:** Assume future employers and internship coordinators will search for your name.
2. **Do fact-check before sharing:** Verify the authenticity of news or forwarding links to prevent the spread of misinformation.
3. **Do use strong privacy settings:** Limit who can view your personal posts, contact details, and location data.
4. **Do engage positively:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to network, share your projects, and support your peers' achievements.
5. **Do separate personal and professional accounts:** Keep platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn strictly for academic/career growth.

## Don'ts
1. **Don't overshare personal details:** Avoid posting your college ID, boarding passes, or real-time location.
2. **Don't engage in cyberbullying:** Never post or forward derogatory, inflammatory, or harmful comments about peers.
3. **Don't click suspicious links:** Be wary of DMs or posts offering free giveaways, as they are often phishing attempts.
4. **Don't post while highly emotional:** Take a step back before posting something in anger or frustration; digital footprints are permanent.
5. **Don't plagiarize content:** Always give proper credit when sharing someone else's art, code, or ideas.
