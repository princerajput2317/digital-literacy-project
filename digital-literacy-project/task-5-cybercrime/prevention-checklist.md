# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

1. **Financial Safety:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money; PINs are only required to *send* money or check your balance.
2. **Financial Safety:** Do not scan unknown QR codes sent via WhatsApp or email, especially from unknown buyers/sellers on marketplaces.
3. **Password Hygiene:** Use a password manager and ensure unique, complex passwords for all university and financial accounts.
4. **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable 2FA on all crucial platforms, including GitHub, Gmail, and social media.
5. **Phishing Awareness:** Always verify the sender's email address. Universities and banks will never ask for your password via email.
6. **Public Wi-Fi Precaution:** Avoid accessing bank accounts or entering sensitive credentials while connected to public college or café Wi-Fi without a VPN.
7. **Software Updates:** Keep your laptop's OS and antivirus software updated to patch vulnerabilities against ransomware.
8. **Digital Footprint:** Regularly audit your social media privacy settings and remove sensitive personal data from public view.

**Official Reporting Channels in India:**
* **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **Cyber Crime Helpline:** Call **1930** (24x7 phone support for financial fraud)
